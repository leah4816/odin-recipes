Salsa Scramble

Delicious Description

This delectable recipe is a fun way to spice up your morning breakfast scramble.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp. sour cream
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • 2 tbsp. salsa

Recipe

  1. Melt butter in pan.
  2. Scramble eggs.
  3. Add sour cream.
  4. Pour scramble in bowl.
  5. Top with salsa.
